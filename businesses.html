LICENSING CODE

The State Licensing Process

All player-owned enterprises must be registered and licensed under the Department of Justice to ensure legal compliance and avoid corporate anti-monopoly violations.

1. Corporate Filing

Draft a detailed business plan including storefront location requirements, services provided, and initial employee payroll plans.

2. DOJ Certification

Submit filings to the DOJ. Attorneys will review the plan to verify licensing codes, state safety regulations, and establish tax registration.

3. Keys Transferred

Once approved, state developers will load your custom interior shell, bind management commands, and transfer the master physical keys.
