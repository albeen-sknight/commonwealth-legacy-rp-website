CIVIL OCCUPATIONS

State-Licensed Occupations

Earn stable salaries working public utility jobs. Secure legal funds to finance import supercars or luxury beach estates.

Cargo Logistics

Patrol state grids shipping commercial stock. Manage long-haul logistics cargo trailers, verify weight scales, and secure custom shipping licenses.

Municipal Sanitation

Work as a crew to secure municipal garbage bins. Meticulous routes that reward cooperative crew coordination with active bonus checks.

Quarry Mining

Excavate rare state minerals at our designated quarry grids. Refine copper, silver, and gold ores, and trade materials at corporate markets.

Sea Salvage & Diving

Equip tactical scuba suits and search underwater reefs. Recover cargo crates, restore vintage shipping parts, and trade assets at state scrap yards.

Wrecker Tow Operations

Respond to citizen vehicular breakdowns. Impound illegal street obstructions, coordinate with State Police, and keep state highways clean.

Parcel Delivery

Ship residential mail and food containers across municipal borders. A simple, fast-paced occupation perfect for starter capital accumulation.
