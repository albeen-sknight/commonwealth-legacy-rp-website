THE LEGAL GRID

A Living System of Law & Consequences

On Commonwealth Legacy RP, illegal progression is structured with substantial depth. Every crime carries forensic trails—fingerprints, spent ammunition shells, and dynamic visual evidence that VSP detectives use to trace operations.

If caught, players enter a robust, serious legal pipeline. You have the right to request active defense counsel, litigate case details in front of Magistrate Judges, or negotiate plea balances. No instant jail warps—actual legal trials.

THE PENAL CODE MATRIX

  • Arrest Registry: Officers catalog files directly into the in-game MDT. Warrants remain active until served.
  • Jail Booking: Administered by VBSO at the local facility. Options to perform labor to shorten sentences.
  • Record Expungement: Clean your background profile by filing civil petitions through licensed DOJ attorneys.
