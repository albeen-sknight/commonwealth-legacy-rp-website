FAQ Index

Have a question about connecting, whitelisting, or setting up positional voice? Browse our indexed answers below or submit a support ticket in Discord.

What is FiveM, and is GTA V required?

FiveM is a custom, fully legal multiplayer modification framework for Grand Theft Auto V. Yes, you must have a legal, licensed copy of GTA V installed on your PC (Steam, Epic Games, or Rockstar launcher) to connect to our server. FiveM does not modify your core GTA files, so you will not be banned from GTA Online.

Does Commonwealth Legacy RP cost money to play?

No. Commonwealth Legacy RP is 100% free to play. We do not sell in-game assets, vehicles, weapons, or properties for real-world money. We are fully funded by optional community server boost donations to secure high-performance host resources and custom asset models.

How long do whitelist applications take to review?

Our dedicated application team reviews registrations continuously. Standard whitelisting applications are processed and graded within 12 hours of submittal. You will receive an automated notification directly on Discord indicating whether your application is approved or requires revision.

Am I allowed to stream my gameplay on Twitch or YouTube?

Absolutely! We encourage content creators to stream their character storylines. However, you must adhere strictly to our Metagaming rules—reading chat messages containing on-stream server secrets or stream-sniping other active players is strictly bannable. We recommend setting a stream delay for tactical operations.

What voice system do we use, and is TeamSpeak required?

No external software like TeamSpeak is required! We utilize the high-performance, native PMA-Voice system integrated directly into FiveM. It supports advanced 3D spatial audio, grid-based distance attenuation, environmental muffling, and fully encrypted dynamic radio frequencies for public service departments—all seamlessly configured within your standard GTA V game settings.
