Virginia Beach Sheriff

Courage, Duty, and Community. Protecting civic sectors, administering the municipal jail, securing legal courts, and keeping community safety intact.

MISSION STATEMENT

Dedicated Community Guardianship

The Virginia Beach Sheriff's Office is committed to enhancing the quality of life in the City of Virginia Beach by providing safe detention operations, professional court security, and responsive community patrol coverage.

We focus on proximity policing, establishing deep community bonds, and maintaining high behavioral standards. Our Academy specializes in county patrols, municipal regulations, and defensive tactical modules.

CORE COMMAND PILLARS

COURAGE: Standing firm in the face of critical dangers to shield local lives.
DUTY: Executing civic orders, detention standards, and patrol beats faithfully.
COMMUNITY: Partnering with civilians to resolve local problems and maintain trust.
SHERIFF'S CAREERS

VBSO Active Divisions

Join unique bureaus. Manage detention, maintain judicial order at courts, or execute warrant missions.

Municipal Patrol

Active grid patrols in urban city zones. Enforcing municipal codes, checking commercial doors, and maintaining local presence.

Detention Services

Administering county jail structures. Processing incoming bookings, managing mugshots, coordinating inmate security, and supervising work details.

Civil Court & Warrant Bureau

Securing the Virginia Beach Courthouse. Preserving order inside, serving court papers, and arresting wanted fugitives.
