CONNECTION PIPELINE

Setup Walkthrough

01

Join The Discord Server

All whitelisting, account synchronization, character approvals, and updates flow directly through our Discord server. You must remain in the Discord to connect to the game server.

Connect To Discord
02

Download FiveM & GTA V

Ensure you have a legal, licensed copy of Grand Theft Auto V installed via Steam, Epic Games, or Rockstar Social Club, then download the official FiveM multiplayer client.

Download FiveM Client
03

Configure In-Game Voice (PMA-Voice)

Commonwealth Legacy RP utilizes FiveM's native, high-immersion PMA-Voice framework. No external software or TeamSpeak downloads are required. Set up your game as follows:

  • ✔ Open Grand Theft Auto V / FiveM and navigate to Settings.
  • ✔ Select Voice Chat and toggle Voice Chat Enabled to On.
  • ✔ Select your correct Input Device (Microphone) and set the volume levels.
  • ✔ Set your Voice Activation Mode to Push to Talk (Default key: N) for immersion.
04

Whitelisting Application

Fill out our integrated roleplay application. Demonstrate your understanding of serious roleplay guidelines, rules limits, and character scenarios.

Open Application Portal
05

Connect and Spawn

Once your application is whitelisted, boot FiveM, ensure your in-game voice chat is enabled, open your console (F8 key), and type the connection string.

connect connect.clrp.gg

Our automatic character loader will boot up, letting you design your ped's facial assets, buy clothing styles, and spawn in Richmond.

PERFORMANCE GUIDANCE

Hardware Specs Recommendation

Commonwealth Legacy RP features optimized custom asset streaming, but serious FiveM roleplay requires stable PC hardware for smooth rendering.

Minimum Configuration

  • CPU: Intel Core i5-4460 / AMD FX-6300
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 (4GB) / AMD Radeon R9 280
  • RAM: 12 GB DDR4
  • Storage: HDD (7200RPM) with GTA V installed
  • System: Windows 10 (64-Bit)

Recommended (For 60FPS+)

  • CPU: Intel Core i7-8700K / AMD Ryzen 5 3600
  • GPU: NVIDIA RTX 2060 (6GB) / AMD RX 5700 XT
  • RAM: 16 GB DDR4 (3200MHz)
  • Storage: SSD (M.2 NVMe highly recommended)
  • System: Windows 11 (64-Bit)
