Virginia State Police

Service, Honor, and Integrity. Maintaining order across state freeways, executing warrants, and executing critical counter-crime operations across the Commonwealth grids.

MISSION STATEMENT

To Shield & Serve the Commonwealth

The Virginia State Police stands as the premier state-level law enforcement body of the Commonwealth. Our primary directive is to provide professional, state-of-the-art policing services, prevent criminal behavior, and enforce state traffic laws with absolute impartiality.

We operate with the highest behavioral guidelines, ensuring all troopers act as leaders within the community. Our Academy is fully structured to transition aspiring cadets into elite state defenders.

CORE COMMAND PILLARS

SERVICE: Prioritizing civilian safety and public welfare above self.
HONOR: Holding our personal and professional actions to standard scrutiny.
INTEGRITY: Adhering to the truth and constitution in all field interventions.
CAREER SPECIALIZATIONS

VSP Specialized Divisions

Once Troopers complete Academy probation, they can apply for specialized bureaus to perform critical tactical duties.

Highway Patrol

Patrolling the arterial interstate systems. Utilizing custom interceptor radar units, managing highway accidents, and running vehicle safety inspections.

Bureau of Investigation

Active plainclothes detectives executing gang task force directives, forensic scene processing, wiretapping, and complex corporate rackets tracking.

Tactical Operations (TOU)

Operating as our elite SWAT squad. Trained to manage armed barricades, active bank heists, hostage rescues, and high-risk counter-terror operations.
