Virginia Emergency Medical

Care, Compassion, and Commitment. Serving the state with rapid emergency trauma care, advanced fire suppression, and marine rescue systems.

MISSION STATEMENT

To Save and Sustain Life

The Virginia Emergency Medical Services is dedicated to providing high-quality, compassionate pre-hospital emergency medical care, technical rescue, and fire suppression to all citizens.

We operate an intensive training regimen including patient triage, skeletal damage stabilization, cardiovascular therapies, and hazard operations. Our Academy equips medics to make critical decisions.

CORE COMMAND PILLARS

CARE: Administering patient treatment with absolute skill and meticulous focus.
COMPASSION: Supporting victims of trauma with empathy, understanding, and respect.
COMMITMENT: Maintaining rapid response status and continuous training vigilance.
MEDICAL CAREERS

VEMS Specialized Divisions

Acquire specialty credentials. Tackle structural fires, lead medical helicopter evacuations, or support SWAT missions.

Trauma Care & EMS

Responding to pre-hospital medical emergency calls. Operating trauma ambulances, triaging bone fractures, and delivering cardiac life support.

Fire Operations

Active structural fire suppression. Utilizing engines, hose rigs, and heavy-duty extraction tools to rescue occupants from wrecked vehicles.

Search & Rescue (SAR)

Conducting sea, air, and wilderness searches. Operating rescue helicopters, deploying shoreline medical crafts, and locating lost civilians.
