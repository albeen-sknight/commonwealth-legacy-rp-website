Department of Justice

Justice, Fairness, and Accountability. Arbitrating civil claims, conducting prosecution, providing defense counsel, and maintaining order under state penal statutes.

MISSION STATEMENT

The Integrity of the Constitution

The Department of Justice ensures that the state's penal codes are applied with absolute fairness, verifying the constitutional rights of all citizens while prosecuting crime with rigorous fidelity.

We operate active municipal courtrooms where legal cases are heard, witnesses are examined, and juries are polled. Our State Bar certifies attorneys, regulating standards of legal counsel across the state.

CORE COMMAND PILLARS

JUSTICE: Deciding legal outcomes purely on facts, logic, and codified penal laws.
FAIRNESS: Ensuring all defendants have access to capable public defense counsel.
INTEGRITY: Verifying search warrants and asset seizures with strict constitutional reviews.

The Judiciary

State Magistrate and Supreme Court Judges. Reviewing warrants, presiding over criminal/civil litigation, setting bail, and handing down penal sentences.

State Prosecution

District Attorneys representing the State. Reviewing arrests, filing formal charges, presenting evidence, and pursuing state sentencing limits.

Public Defense & Bar

Public defenders representing citizens. The State Bar Association certifies legal licenses, sets attorney requirements, and catalogs business corporations.
